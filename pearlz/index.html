Deployment
Service

Pearlz Certified platforms and services make securing and enabling compliance of your hosted infrastructure and applications a lot easier. Our compliance framework is built on best practices to address the most stringent controls for physical, network/perimeter, continuous monitoring, system host, and physical access to facilities. Furthermore, DataBank’s in-house CISO, dedicated security engineering teams, and proven compliance expertise enable us to take ownership of up to 80% of compliance controls, whereas other providers may only provide 10%.

Compliance
Services

To keep your infrastructure safe, secure, and compliant, you need a multi-layered, defense-in-depth approach backed by professionals who are ready to respond at a moment’s notice. This is why we provide a broad range of managed security services that you can mix and match to complement your hybrid IT infrastructure. For every solution we deliver, our five-layer defense approach provides comprehensive protection and includes a dedicated team of security engineers and a seasoned CISO.

Data Protection
Services

Your entire business is data in the digital economy, and every byte must be safeguarded. However, it is no longer just about data backup; it is also about data recovery. We assist you in developing a recovery and backup strategy by combining proven platforms and technologies with managed compliance and security services. We back up, replicate, and recover your files while meeting FedRAMP, HIPAA/HITECH, PCI-DSS, SSAE 18, FISMA, and GDPR – Privacy Shield compliance requirements.
